#Markdown Syntax and formatting 

---------
#Headings
---------

#Use hashtags for headers
# Heading <h1>
## Heading <h2>
### Heading <h3>
#### Heading <h4>
##### Heading <h5>
###### Heading <h6>

---------------------------
#Bold, Italic and Underline
---------------------------

Use asterisks to **bold** text
Use asterisk to *italicize* text
Use underscore to _italicize_ text
